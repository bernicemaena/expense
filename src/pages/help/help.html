E-wallet is an income and expense tracking mobile app that helps you budget, track your spending, and reach your financial goals. Below is a guide to help you get started with E-wallet.

Step 1:Create new account with E-wallet

Sign up with your email address to get unlimited access to your account for free.

Step 2: Track income and expenses

Tracking your income and expenses helps you understand how much money you have to work with and where your money is going. You can manually add cash transactions and assign a category.

Step 3: Analyse expenses

You can also view your expenses in charts and graphs on the dashboard to help you visualize your data.

Step 4: Create your budget

On the navigation menu, click on the budget icon and enter your budget details here. You can choose to edit/delete budget and also being notified when you've overspent or when you risk overspending.
